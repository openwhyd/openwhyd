To install the bookmarklet in your browser:

  1. In Chrome, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Always Show Bookmarks Bar
  2. In Firefox, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar
  3. In Safari, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Show Bookmarks Bar
  4. In Internet Explorer, display your bookmarks bar by clicking Tools > Toolbars > Favorites Bar
  5. Drag this button, and drop it in your bookmarks bar
  6. While browsing the web, click the bookmarklet whenever you see some music you want to add to your library
