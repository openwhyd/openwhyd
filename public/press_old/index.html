Press Info

Product

Openwhyd is a free social music platform designed for music lovers to collect and share all of the internet's music. It is the missing link between places where people post music to stream, like YouTube and Soundcloud, and the places where people share music, like Facebook, and Twitter. Since streaming music is only in its infancy, Openwhyd is positioned to eventually help everyone enjoy the best of the newest music.

Collect

Openwhyd lets music lovers aggregate links to music from all over the web, and organize them into playlists.

  • Add Track Button: searches all of the platforms supported by Whyd, including YouTube, Soundcloud, Vimeo, MP3 blogs, etc to find almost any song, especially rare cuts, new remixes, and even performances.
  • Openwhyd Bookmarklet/Chrome Extension: simple install for a web browser that lets users add songs to their Openwhyd page from any other site where it is embedded or hosted, like Facebook, in two clicks.
  • Listen to anyone's playlists, whether you are logged in or not.
  • Find any song you could desire, it is an unlimited library.
  • Play music from multiple sources seamlessly, mix playlist from YouTube, Soundcloud, Vimeo, etc.

Share

Openwhyd is a social network designed for music lovers. Sharing is entirely integrated into the listening and adding experience.

  • Every track added means a track shared, there are no private playlists.
  • Follow interesting music lovers with similar taste in music to customize your incoming stream. Whenever people you subscribe to add a track, it comes up in your stream. It is automated and endless music discovery.
  • Share specific tracks or playlists to Facebook or Twitter
  • Embed your Openwhyd playlist on other sites with the playlist embed, used by blogs, concert venues and others in the music space to present music for their audiences. Anyone can listen to these embedded playlists, and they take less than a minute to create.

Openwhyd community

Openwhyd is utilized by music lovers around the world. Indie record labels, music blogs, concert venues, and DJs use Openwhyd to share tracks with the community and to embed the playlist. Combined, our community is the filter that brings the best music from the web to the platform everyday. It's how music lovers can find the coolest new tracks online when the number of tracks shared everyday continues to increase.

Differentiation

There are a lot of streaming music services out there, why exactly is Openwhyd different?

  • Community - Our community is a music lovers community built from music industry people, labels, promoters, club owners, musicians and DJ's. If you love music - you belong on Openwhyd (see above).
  • Curation made easy - if you love music, you have a collection. Openwhyd is your tool to save, play and share music from multiple sources.
  • Differentiation from premium services - with Openwhyd you can access all mainstream music, but also the long tail of music: live versions, covers, Bootlegs, remixes, 2 hours DJ sets, concerts etc. stuff you'll never find on premium music services.
