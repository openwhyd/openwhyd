Openwhyd is the platform for music lovers to keep, play, and share all of the internet’s music, from emerging groups to the classics. We build a playlist embed for concert venues to showcase upcoming bands and artists by combining all of the places where artists share their music in one place that anyone can listen to regardless of geographic limitations.

Why Should I use it?

    • Fast

    • - Make and embed a playlist in under a minute
    • - Bookmark tracks to your embed automatically while you surf the web
    • - Manage your playlist on Openwhyd to keep it fresh

    • Awesome

    • - Share the great bands coming to your area with the world, showcase their talent!
    • - Improve the quality of your site with media that will make people want to visit often
    • - Share new music with the Openwhyd Community automatically

    • Free

    • - It's totally and completely free

Who else uses it?

  • Music Like Dirt - UK Music Blog
