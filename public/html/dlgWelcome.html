Welcome to Openwhyd!

Openwhyd is a simple way to keep, play and share all the tracks you find on Youtube, Soundcloud, Hype Machine, etc...

Add a first track to your library

Click on the green add button, search for your track, and add it to your library in 2 clicks!

Add a track from another user

Listen to the tracks posted by other users, and add the ones you love to your own library, instantly.
