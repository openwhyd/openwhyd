The fastest way to add and share

To instantly add tracks to your Whyd stream that you find across the web, from any website, we built the Whyd "add track" button. Drag the button to your bookmarks bar to install it, and then you can save the new tracks you find and share immediately from sites like Facebook and music blogs. It's super fast and super simple.
How to install the button ?

Works with tracks from

  • Vimeo

  • & many more coming...

All the cool kids are doing it!

Instructions:

  • In Chrome, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Always Show Bookmarks Bar
  • In Firefox, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar
  • In Safari, display your bookmarks bar by clicking View > Show Bookmarks Bar
  • In Internet Explorer 8, display your bookmarks bar by clicking Tools > Toolbars > Favorites Bar
  • In Internet Explorer, display your bookmarks bar by right-clicking the top grey area of the browser, then click on Favorites Bar
  • Drag this button, and drop it in your bookmarks bar
  • While browsing the web, click on "Whyd + Track" whenever you see some music you want to add to your library
  • Or, install our brand new Whyd Button extension for Chrome.
